About the Developer

Solo.Prodigy is an independent game developer who just really likes making cool stuff for people to play. His goal is simple: create fun and enjoyable games that players actually feel are worth buying. Nothing fancy. Just good vibes and good gameplay.

Before starting Shadewalker he made a couple of absolutely legendary school projects: Serein and OverStory (DEFINITELY NOT AN UNDERTALE RIPOFF), both on itch.io, both 3D, and both powered by an unhealthy amount of copyrighted assets because he was too lazy to make his own at the time (Still is). Those projects will not be going professional anytime soon, but they taught him a lot about game dev and how not to get sued.

Shadewalker is his first real professional game (I think?..). It is built from scratch, properly licensed, fully original, and genuinely something he is proud of. Every system, animation, and idea comes from learning, improving, and wanting to make something players will actually enjoy.

He might be young, but Solo.Prodigy is aiming to grow as a developer, keep making creative games, and prove that anyone can build something awesome if they just start and don't stop.
