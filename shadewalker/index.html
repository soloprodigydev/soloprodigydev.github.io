About the Game

"In a world where light is lethal, only the shadows offer refuge."

You play as Draxis Veylor, a man feared and mythologized as the Shadewalker ‚Äî a figure who moves with the darkness, hunts in silence, and survives where others burn. Draxis carries a rare curse that lets him separate from his own shadow, creating a living double born from pure darkness.

This ability, known as the Shadow Split, is the heart of the game: a fast, mind-bending mechanic that lets players control both body and shadow to outmaneuver enemies, solve dual-perspective puzzles, and navigate impossible challenges. Shadewalker blends stealth, exploration, and atmospheric storytelling into a world where every ray of light is a threat ‚Äî and mastering your own shadow is the only way to survive.

Shadewalker is a 2D platformer developed by Solo.Prodigy using the Godot game engine. This project draws inspiration from two legendary titles: Undertale by Toby Fox and Hollow Knight by Team Cherry.
