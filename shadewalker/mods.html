Modding Guidelines

We love the modding community and encourage players to create and share their own content for Shadewalker. These guidelines explain what's allowed, what's not, and how to safely share your creations with the world.

What's Allowed

  • Free mods only (no paid content)
  • Texture, audio, and UI replacements
  • New levels, enemies, or gameplay tweaks
  • Quality-of-life improvements
  • Custom difficulty modes
  • Safe, respectful, non-harmful content
  • Mods using CC0 assets or original work
  • Content clearly marked as unofficial
‚úó

What's Not Allowed

  • Paid or monetized mods
  • Hateful, violent, or illegal content
  • Story leaks or datamined assets
  • Redistribution of original game code
  • Malicious code or exploits
  • Copyrighted material without permission
  • Mods that harm the game's integrity
  • Commercial derivatives or clones

Distribution Rules

  • Mods must be shared completely free of charge
  • Clearly state that your mod is unofficial and fan-made
  • Never reupload the full game or substantial portions
  • Include a readme file with installation instructions
  • Credit any third-party assets you use (if allowed)
  • Respect the EULA and these modding guidelines
  • Be respectful to other modders and the community
